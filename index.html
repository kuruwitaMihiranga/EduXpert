About the Product

This project aims to develop a software system that might enhance the effectiveness of distant learning by removing the question areas. Using an iris tracking method, the program has a study tracker capable of capturing studentsâ€™ behavior during class hours. A dynamic question generator is designed to aid students through the process of learning by them. A viva automation tool that can replace the online viva examination procedure via speech recognition and a document simplifier that will help in lexical simplification using a web scraper and reinforcementlearning unit. Existing shortcomings in current learning management systems will be addressed by a revolutionary system with these components.

    Research Gap

    Eduxpert software solution introduces novel features which will help the student community, to learn while tracking their progress and helping them to conduct self assessments on themselves, which in-turn increase the efficiency of distance learning. By the survey that has been conducted, our team was able to identify pressing issues that hinder the online learning process. Hence the proposing EduXpert software solution, may produce and distribute information while monitoring students, and students can comprehend knowledge as receivers , while existing systems provide basic requirements that are expected from such a system, novel features are needed to cater to new functional requirements. The Study tracker , question generator, viva automation tool and document simplifier are the novel components of this Eduxpert. Hence, the research is focused on implementing four unique components to meet the gap in existing learning management software systems.

    Research Problem

    E-learning is a segment that is now experiencing both robust demand and growth across the nation. Due to the political environment in the country at the time, beginning at the end of 2019, all elementary, middle, and high school students were taught online. The majority of these online instruction tactics are ineffective, and only a few may be considered successful.Due to its online nature and teachers' reluctance to emphasize it in the classroom, students' interest in learning through various online platforms, such as Zoom, is currently waning. This trend's essential explanation is. There is reluctance to listen to the lengthy lecture, and a portion of what is being taught is difficult to comprehend. It is difficult for students engaged in online learning to work with their professors to discover solutions to problems that develop during their study. In addition, the instructor does not devote as much time to each student as they would during physical sessions, and it is difficult to keep track of what the instructor is teaching the students at any one time. Because it is quite probable that teachers employ this physical learning method due to cultural considerations.
